About Us

Cryptik Computers started when a group of best friends in first year of college decided to put together their likes for PC gaming and skills of repairing and building computers and opened a business to generate some income.
From building custom computers, refurbishing and repairing to custom designing of cases.
This group could do it all.
To advertise their skills, they decided to create a website to display their work and services for ease of access to almost anyone.
They currently plan to expand the website into a full walk in store where customers can get computers build same-day, repairs done on spot and a facility to store their custom work.
